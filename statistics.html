üí° Economic Context & Interpretation

Why Gold Responds to Inflation:

  • Store of Value: As currency purchasing power decreases during inflation, investors seek tangible assets like gold
  • Limited Supply: Unlike fiat currency, gold cannot be printed, maintaining its scarcity value
  • Historical Precedent: Gold has maintained purchasing power across millennia, surviving currency collapses
  • Central Bank Behavior: During high inflation, central banks increase gold reserves, driving demand
  • Real Returns: When inflation exceeds interest rates (negative real rates), gold becomes more attractive

Practical Applications:

  • Portfolio diversification during inflationary periods
  • Hedging against currency devaluation
  • Long-term wealth preservation strategy
  • Indicator for economic policy changes
